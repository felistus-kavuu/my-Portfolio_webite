About the project

Project summary

I worked on this project during a five week Hackathon known as the Game of Learners. I worked in a team with four other students to build a web application. The theme of the hackathon was Climate Change and Sustainability.

The problem

The reason greenhouse gas emission and climate change continues to be a global issue is because people do not have the necessary knowledge. They don't know the impact their decisions have had and how they can combat those effects. Our aim is to change that.

What the project does

Carbon Sprint gives our users the platform to learn using fun games and articles on climate change. Users will also be able to join challenges geared towards reducing greenhouse gas emissions. We have also integrated a carbon footprint calculator that will provide users with information on how much greenhouse gas they are emitting based on their transport means choices.

How we built the project

We used html, CSS and JavaScript to build our game and design various homepage buttons. We also integrated a chatbot using the Power Virtual Agents to help users in navigation through the app by answering frequently asked questions.
